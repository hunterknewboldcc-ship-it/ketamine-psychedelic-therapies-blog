Critical Patient Guidance

Before contacting any provider, review these important considerations

‚úì Verify Licensing

Confirm the prescriber is licensed in Utah through the DOPL lookup system.

‚úì Clarify Costs

Ketamine therapy is often cash-pay. Ask about total protocol cost, not just per-session pricing.

‚úì Ask About Integration

Effective treatment includes preparation and post-session therapy. Does the provider offer this?

‚úì Emergency Disclaimer

Ketamine and psychedelic therapies are not crisis interventions. If in acute distress, contact 988 or go to the nearest ER.
